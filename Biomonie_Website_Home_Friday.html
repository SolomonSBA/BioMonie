Frequently Asked Questions

Questions?
We have answers.

Everything you need to know about BIOMONIE, the referral programme, and how to get started.

QDo I need a bank account to use BIOMONIE?

No. Your BIOWALLET does not replace your bank accounts. It's just a wallet for everyday payments and cash needs. You can transfer in and out, deposit, withdraw, and pay using BIOMONIE through Agents, Merchants, or the App.

QIs it really free to join?

Absolutely. No registration fees, no subscription fees, no hidden charges. Anyone who asks you to pay money to join BIOMONIE is not authorised.

QHow exactly do I earn as an Affiliate BRM?

Share your referral code and ensure everyone you introduce uses it. You earn once they complete their 200th transaction for referral-based earnings (₦3,000 — only applicable to Agents and Merchants). After that, you earn ₦500 for every subsequent block of 75 transactions — with no limit and no expiry.

QWhen do I get paid?

Your earnings are calculated and paid into your BIOWALLET once you achieve your applicable transaction earning threshold or referral earning threshold — instantly, as you meet each milestone.

QIs my money and data safe?

BIOMONIE is licensed as a processor. Your data is encrypted and stored.

QCan I earn from the people my referrals refer?

No. BIOMONIE uses a Single Level Downline Model (SLD) — you earn only from the people you personally introduce. You do not earn from their referrals.

QWhat kind of businesses can be Merchants?

Any business — not just small businesses. If they sell goods or services and accept money from customers, BIOMONIE works for them. Large or small, the ecosystem welcomes all.

QAre there physical cards involved?

No. BIOMONIE makes YOU the money access. It offers the new form of money access — YOU.

QAre there POS terminals involved?

No. The BIOMONIE ecosystem leverages mobile phones, with a proprietary app that allows the customer to be the new form of money access. No POS terminal, no card reader, no hardware to manage.
